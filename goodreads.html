goodreads

Redesign

  • About:

    This is a project completed at The Iron Yard to redesign the goodreads.com home feed. The current goodreads site is used as a form of social media to connect with others to share their reading experiences. The home feed is used to share individual posts, view your friends' posts, and view you books. It was my goal to take the current site layout and make it more user friendly.

  • Objective:

    My primary objectives were to decrease the noise from the original site to make it cleaner and more organized, change the color pallette to make the site more welcoming, and make the social feed function of the site more obvious to the user.
